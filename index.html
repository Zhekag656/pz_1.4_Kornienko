George Orwell "1984"

A dystopia by the English writer George Orwell, written in 1948 (the numbers 4 and 8 are reversed in the title of the novel) and published in 1949. The novel tells the story of Winston Smith and his degradation under the influence of the totalitarian state in which he lives. It is the most famous and most quoted dystopia in literature and ranks with Aldous Huxley's Brave New World and Ray Bradbury's Fahrenheit 451...
